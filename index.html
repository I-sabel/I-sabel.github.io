Pit Stop Café blends the excitement of Formula One with the cozy relaxation of a traditional cat café. The café operates as both a comfortable spot for customers to enjoy specialty coffee and snacks while interacting with rescue cats, and a motorsport fan hub where racing enthusiasts can catch live race screenings, play racing-themed board games, and shop for F1-themed merchandise. The café partners with local shelters, promoting cat adoption through special "Box Box" events where adoptable cats get featured.

Pit Stop Café Team Principal

Olivia "Ollie" Hamilton

Ollie is a former motorsport journalist with a passion for Formula One racing and an equally strong love for cats. After years of covering Formula One events around the world, she decided to combine her two passions into a unique business concept. She's known for her energetic personality, love for speed, and quirky sense of humor, which she brings into the café's branding and atmosphere. Ollie is often found chatting with customers about racing history while surrounded by the café's resident cats, each named after famous F1 drivers.
